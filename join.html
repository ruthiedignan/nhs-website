How to Join

Requirements

Must hold and maintain a GPA of 3.4 or higher

Willing to be committed to a regular meeting schedule

NHS meetings are almost always two Thursdays per month, with potential additional meetings for projects

Must complete a minimum of 15 hours of community service per semester (view the COVID-19 Update page for details regarding service hours for the 2020-21 school year)

Joining Process

1. If you are a sophomore or junior with a GPA of 3.4 or higher, you are automatically given an invitation towards the beginning of the year to join the NHS informational meeting held in the winter.

2. Attending the NHS informational meeting is required if you desire to join NHS, however, attending the meeting does not mean you are required to join NHS. If you are unable to attend the meeting, please speak to Mrs. Laskowski to negotiate an accommodation.

3. All attendees will be given an essay prompt, as well as a list of the four possible NHS committees to choose from. Completing seven hours of service is also required (view the COVID-19 Update page for details regarding service hours for the 2020-21 school year). If you are interested in joining, these tasks must be completed by a specified date.

4. Acceptances will be handed out and incoming members will be inducted at a ceremony, projected to be in early March. Subsequent to the ceremony, new members will be able to attend their first meeting as an official NHS member.
