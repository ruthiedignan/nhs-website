Who Are We?

The 2020 NHS executive board at the induction ceremony. Taken by Historian Olivia Frey. 4 March 2020.

The Owosso High School National Honor Society is a student organization comprised of hardworking students who are dedicated to giving back to the community, working to make the school environment positive, and encouraging all to be thankful for their surroundings.

NHS members work regularly on a variety of projects, constantly striving for success and achievement. Members are consistently honoring their peers, staff members, community members, and more with traditions of excellence.

To learn more about what NHS does to serve our community, please visit the About Us page. View the COVID-19 Updates page for all atypical alterations made regarding 2020-2021 school year.
